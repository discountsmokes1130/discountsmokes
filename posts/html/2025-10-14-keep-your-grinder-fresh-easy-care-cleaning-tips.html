Care Tips for Your Grinder

  1. Regular Cleaning:
    Cleaning your grinder every few weeks is a good rule of thumb. Simply disassemble the grinder and use a small brush or a toothpick to remove any leftover herb particles. For a thorough clean, soak the pieces in isopropyl alcohol for about 30 minutes, then rinse with warm water. Make sure to let all components dry completely before reassembling!

  2. Avoiding Sticky Situations:
    If you notice that your grinder is becoming sticky, consider storing your herbs in a cool, dry place. Moisture can cause buildup, making it harder to grind. A silicone mat can also help reduce residue and make cleaning easier.
