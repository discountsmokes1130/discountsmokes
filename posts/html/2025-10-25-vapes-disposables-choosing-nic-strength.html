Factors to Consider

Your personal preferences and habits play a significant role in determining the right nicotine strength for you. Consider how often you vape, the type of flavors you enjoy, and your overall goals. Additionally, the type of device you use can influence your nicotine choice. For example, sub-ohm vapes typically produce larger vapor clouds and may require lower nicotine levels, while pod systems designed for mouth-to-lung vaping can accommodate higher strengths comfortably.
