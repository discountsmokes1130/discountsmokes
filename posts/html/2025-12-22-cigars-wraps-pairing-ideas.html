Elevate Your Cigar Experience with Perfect Pairings

At Discount Smokes, we know that choosing the right cigar or wrap is just the beginning of your enjoyment. Pairing your selection with the right beverage or snack can take the experience to a whole new level! Here in Westport, we have some fantastic local options that complement your cigar or wrap perfectly.

Beverage Pairings to Consider

When it comes to beverages, the world is your oyster! Here are a few local favorites to try:

  • Craft Beer: Kansas City is known for its vibrant craft beer scene. A rich stout or a hoppy IPA can create a delightful contrast to a smooth cigar. For a local touch, consider picking up a brew from Boulevard Brewing Company, just a short drive away. Their "6th Glass" Quadrupel Ale pairs beautifully with a robust cigar.

  • Whiskey: If you prefer something a bit stronger, a glass of bourbon is a classic pairing. Westport has several distilleries, and you can find some fantastic local whiskeys that add a rich, warming note to your smoke. Try a glass of Kansas City's own J. Rieger & Co. whiskey, which offers notes of vanilla and caramel that blend seamlessly with your cigar's flavors.

  • Coffee: For a non-alcoholic option, consider pairing your cigar with a freshly brewed cup of coffee. Westport boasts plenty of cozy cafes where you can grab a quality brew. The rich, bold flavors of a dark roast coffee can enhance the complexities of your cigar, providing a perfect balance.

Snack Pairings to Enhance Your Enjoyment

Don't forget about snacks! Munching on the right treat can enhance your smoking experience. Here are a few local favorites:
