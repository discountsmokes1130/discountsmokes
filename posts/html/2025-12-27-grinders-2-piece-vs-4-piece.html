Understanding 2-Piece Grinders

2-piece grinders are the simplest design available. They consist of two interlocking pieces that allow you to grind your herbs effectively. One of the main benefits of a 2-piece grinder is its ease of use. With fewer parts to handle, they are straightforward for beginners and convenient for on-the-go use. Which Grinder is Right for You?

Ultimately, the choice between a 2-piece and a 4-piece grinder boils down to your personal preferences and how you plan to use it.
